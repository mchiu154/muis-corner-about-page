A bit about myself- I am a self-taught nail tech who’s nail journey began in 2020. I used to get my nails done monthly, but similarly to others, when the pandemic struck, I resorted to providing the service myself! Doing my own nails has quickly became a form a therapy that I resort to when life gets overwhelming. It’s allocated time where I get to put on my favourite playlist, sit down for a few hours and focus on creating art. The name of my business- mui’s corner, represents me as a brand. In my family, I am the youngest, and mui translates to younger sister in Cantonese. mui’s corner is a place where I am able to express myself freely through nail design, and I welcome you to relax, pull up a chair, and join me in my little corner :)
